Cats

Cats are small and cute animals that many people love. They have soft fur, sharp eyes, and long tails. Cats can live in homes or outside, and they like to play and sleep a lot. Cats are very smart and clean animals. They help people by catching mice and keeping houses clean. Many people keep cats as pets because they are friendly and fun.

How to Deal with Cats

Cats are gentle animals and need kind treatment. We should always be calm when dealing with cats and never hurt them. Speaking softly helps cats feel safe and comfortable. Cats need proper care every day. We should give them clean food and fresh water, and keep their place clean. Playing with cats makes them happy and helps them stay active.
